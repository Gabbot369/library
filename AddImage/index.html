About Us

Our QR Memory Album is a seamless way of preserving cherished memories with their own personalized QR code. Customers are able to upload videos and pictures, all securely stored digitally, so their special moments are always accessible. Our product is convenient and digitally stores your photos without stressing over large albums and disorganized digital files. Scan the QR code and log in to relive your memories anywhere and at any time.

Vision

To be the leading digital memory preservation solution in the Philippines, blending technology with nostalgia to help people cherish and share their most precious moments forever.


Mission

Striving to build strong brand recognition in Cebu City through engaging marketing strategies, excellent customer service, and continuous product improvement. By leveraging technology and the customer's insights, we aim to create a meaningful and lasting impact on how people relive and celebrate their cherished memories.

Goals

  1. Build strong brand awareness and make the QR Memory Album a well-known product in Cebu City.
  2. Build a strong customer base by ensuring high satisfaction and positive feedback, establishing trust and credibility for long-term growth.
  3. Within the first two years, extend the product's availability to other major cities in the Philippines through online sales.
